Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025. We've made this as clear as possible — read the section that applies to your purchase. If you still have questions, email us before buying.

📋 Quick Summary

  • Digital reports: 7-day full refund if the PDF has not been downloaded yet.
  • Consulting sessions: full refund with 24+ hours notice; 50% refund with less than 24 hours notice; no refund for no-shows.
  • Alert subscription: cancel anytime — no partial-month refunds after the billing date.
  • No shipping costs — ever. All products and services are digital. Nothing is shipped.
  • Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.
  • To request a refund: email hello@cryptolens.io with your order details.

1. Digital Market Reports

Topic Policy
Refund window Refund requests must be submitted within 7 calendar days of the date of purchase. Requests received after 7 days will not be eligible for a refund.
Eligibility condition The report must not have been downloaded at the time the refund is requested. Once you download the PDF, the sale is considered final. This is standard practice for digital goods — comparable to Amazon's policy on downloaded Kindle books or downloaded software.
Why no refund after download? Unlike a physical product, a downloaded PDF cannot be "returned." Once you've read the content, we have no way to verify it hasn't been copied. We've priced our reports fairly to reflect this. If you're unsure a report is right for you, email us first — we're happy to describe the contents in more detail before you buy.
How to request a refund Email hello@cryptolens.io with the subject line "Refund Request — [Report Name]". Include the email address used to place the order. We'll confirm receipt within 1 business day.
Refund amount Full purchase price — no deductions, no restocking fees, no handling fees.
Refund timeline Once approved, refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method (PayPal or Venmo). You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is issued.
Return shipping Not applicable. All reports are digital PDFs. There is nothing to ship or return. No customer will ever be asked to pay any shipping cost.
Partial refunds Not available. Reports are either fully refundable (if not downloaded within 7 days) or non-refundable (if downloaded). There is no partial refund option.
Defective or incorrect file If you received a corrupted file, a file that won't open, or a report that is clearly not what was described, we will replace it within 24 hours at no charge or issue a full refund — your choice. Email us immediately with a description of the issue.
Duplicate purchases If you accidentally purchased the same report twice (within 7 days), we'll refund the duplicate immediately, no questions asked.

2. Consulting Sessions

Scenario Refund Amount & Process
Cancellation with 24+ hours notice 100% full refund. No questions asked. Simply reply to your booking confirmation email with the subject "Cancellation Request" at least 24 hours before the scheduled session start time. Refund is processed within 5–10 business days.
Cancellation with less than 24 hours notice 50% refund. The remaining 50% compensates for the reserved time slot, which cannot be offered to another client at short notice. The refund is processed within 5–10 business days after cancellation is confirmed.
No-show (you don't join) No refund. If you fail to join a scheduled session without prior cancellation, the session is considered complete and no refund is issued. If an emergency caused the no-show, contact us — we may offer a one-time complimentary reschedule at our discretion.
Analyst no-show or early end (our fault) Full refund or free reschedule — your choice. If the CryptoLens analyst fails to appear or ends the session more than 15 minutes early due to technical issues on our end, you are entitled to either a 100% refund or a rescheduled session at no additional charge. We will contact you proactively if this occurs.
How to cancel Reply to your booking confirmation email with the subject line "Cancellation Request." Your cancellation is not confirmed until you receive a reply from us acknowledging it. Do not assume the session is cancelled without this confirmation.
Rescheduling Rescheduling with 24+ hours notice is free and unlimited. Reschedule by replying to your booking email with your new preferred date and time.
Refund timeline 5–10 business days to the original payment method.
Return shipping Not applicable. Consulting is delivered entirely via video call. No physical goods are involved, and no shipping costs exist.

3. Monthly Market Alert Subscription

Topic Policy
Cancellation Cancel anytime by emailing hello@cryptolens.io. There is no minimum subscription term, no cancellation fee, and no lock-in period.
When does cancellation take effect? Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. For example, if you subscribed on the 5th and cancel on the 20th, your alerts continue until the 5th of the following month. You will not be charged again.
Partial-month refunds No partial-month refunds. Once a billing period has started, that month's charge is non-refundable. You retain access through the end of the period. This is standard practice for subscription services.
Billing Subscriptions are billed monthly on the same calendar date you originally subscribed. If that date doesn't exist in a given month (e.g., the 31st), billing occurs on the last day of the month.
Service interruption credit If the alert service is disrupted for more than 5 consecutive days in a billing period due to issues on our end, we'll extend your subscription by 7 days at no charge as compensation.
Return shipping Not applicable. Alert emails are digital. No physical product is involved.
📦 A Note on Shipping — Required Disclosure CryptoLens sells exclusively digital products and services. There is no physical merchandise, no inventory, and no supply chain. No customer will ever be charged for shipping, return shipping, handling, or postage of any kind — under any circumstances. This section exists solely to be transparent: if you ever see a shipping charge associated with CryptoLens, it is an error and should be reported to us immediately.

5. How to Request a Refund — Step by Step

  1. Email hello@cryptolens.io with the subject line: "Refund Request — [Your Purchase]" (e.g., "Refund Request — Altcoin Season Watchlist").
  2. In the email body, include: your full name, the email address used to purchase, the product or session you're requesting a refund for, and the reason for your request (optional, but helpful).
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 1 business day. If you don't hear back within 2 business days, please follow up — occasionally emails end up in spam filters.
  4. Once your request is reviewed and approved, we will process the refund within 5–10 business days to your original payment method (PayPal or Venmo).
  5. You'll receive a final confirmation email when the refund has been issued. PayPal/Venmo processing times may add 1–3 additional business days before it appears in your account.
This policy was last reviewed and updated in January 2025. CryptoLens reserves the right to update this policy at any time — the version at this URL is always the most current. Purchases made before a policy update are governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase. If you have any questions about this policy before making a purchase, please email hello@cryptolens.io — we'd rather answer your questions upfront than have you be surprised later.
